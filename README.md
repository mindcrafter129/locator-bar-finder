# Locator Bar Finder
### A tool for modern (1.21.6+) versions of minecraft that alllows you to determine the position of a player with just the locator bar.

## Tutorial

**Step 1**: Look at the target in the middle.

![locator bar: ===============O=============== ](https://images.paymind.online/locator-bar-minecraft.webp)


Then, press F3 + C once, **don't hold as this will crash your game**.

![don't hold f3 + c as it will crash your game](https://images.paymind.online/f3c-crash-minecraft.webp)

**Step 2**: Move

Move enough blocks to get a good enough reading (more is better) and press F3 + C

**Step 3**: Repeat until satisfied.

Repeat Step 1-2 until you get enough readings that you're satisfied.

**Step 4**: Change to y mode

Focus on the app and press Y.

**Step 5**: Look down
Then slowly go up until the arrow disappears.

![Look down, then slowly go up until the down arrow](https://images.paymind.online/locator-bar-arrow-up.webp)

**Step 6**: Repeat on top side.
